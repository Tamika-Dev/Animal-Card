White Tiger

White tigers are born in one in 10,000 tiger births.

  • Scentific Name: Panthera Tigris
  • Average Length: 8.9ft - 10ft
  • Average Lifestyle: 10-20 years
  • Habitat: Tropical Jungles, mangrove swamps and grasslands near fresh water

White tigers get their color from a mutation called leucism. This mutation occurs as a result of two tigers who have the recessive gene called allele mating. There are currently no white tigers in the wild. Only around 200 white tigers exist in the world today.
